Shrimp Pasta.

I could eat this all day everyday!

Ingredients needed

  • Butter
  • Shrimp
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Nokoss
  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt

Recipe

  1. First season shrimp with nokoss.
  2. Then pour oil in pan.
  3. When oil is, hot pour butter into the pan.
  4. Get about 10 pieces of garlic, smash them then throw it in buttter.
  5. Put shrimp in and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  6. Pour a good amount of water, close pan and let the shrimp cook.
  7. In another pot boil water.
  8. When water is boiled, put salt then pasta.
  9. Drain the water,put butter in your pasta.
  10. When you see the shrimp forming sauce turn the stove off.
  11. Serve your food.
